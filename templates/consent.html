Research Study: AI-Assisted Learning

About this study

You are invited to take part in a short research study on how students interact with AI tutoring systems. You will take a brief quiz on machine learning concepts, with an AI tutor available to assist you.

Your responses and interactions with the tutor will be recorded for research purposes. The session takes approximately 15 minutes. You may stop at any time.

After the quiz, you will receive a debriefing with additional information about the study.

By proceeding, you confirm that you are 18 or older and consent to participate.
